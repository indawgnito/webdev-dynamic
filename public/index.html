San Andreas Fault Data

  Our website showcases the data from the San Andreas Fault dataset , which we found on the FiveThirtyEight repository on github. We are looking at how people's age, income, and region affect how concerned they are about experiencing an earthquake. We additionally compare this against how many of each group has actually experienced an earthquake.
